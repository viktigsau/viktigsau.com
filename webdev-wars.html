Introducing WebDev Wars

What is WebDev Wars?

WebDev Wars is a hackatoon where we make websites. We accept all kinds of websites, including games, as long as they are written in HTML, CSS, JS, TS, PHP, or any other language the web is made of. And as a bonus, it's completely free.

Where to submit code?

You can submit your code by sending an email to webdevwars@viktigsau.org with the code. If your website is hard to understand or interpret, you may include a description or explanation.

What is the prize for winning?

The prize for winning is that the site gets put on a list on my website for peapole to see. your name and contact info will to be there if you want.

When?

we will be announcing the winner on the October 31st at 6 PM GMT+01:00. In the Young Programmer Society discord.
